Thank you for becoming a Library of Congress virtual transcription volunteer for the Library of Congress!

Before you jump in, read the Get Started guide. It includes instructions on transcribing and reviewing transcriptions by other volunteers. Reviewing is an area where we especially need your help to complete transcriptions!

Once you have a handle on the transcription guidelines, explore the different collections available under “Campaigns” using the top navigation on any page.

Let us know what you find once you dig in! Share your experience or questions on our community forum.
